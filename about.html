About Us

It was year 2007, when two enthusiastic individuals, Dr. Ashok Bhatia and Mr. Satyan Bhatia, decided to embark on a journey towards creating a better world. A world of health, wealth and comfort. And, in this quest, they started IMC, a direct selling company, which deals in organic Ayurvedic patented herbal, health, beauty and personal care products. Over the years, the organisation has made a phenomenal advancement towards its goal by making individuals financially independent with their diligent marketing model and creating a healthier world with their finest organic products.

A proud member of FICCI, the Company always aims at rendering the right kind of services for the betterment of society and the world. Owing to their efforts to build a sustainable and rewarding environment all around, the Company has been appreciated and recognised at various occasions. With thousands of happy associates and millions of delighted customers, the Company has become a trusted household name in India and is currently expanding its horizon by marking its presence in other parts of the world.

Since, IMC focuses on promoting the heritage of India along with augmenting health and providing easy livelihood to everyone, it introduced the concept of AloeVeda, which is a concoction of the goodness of Aloe Vera and the age-old science of Ayurveda. Under this concept, the Company has developed a number of unprecedented products that would reinstate everyone’s belief in nature and its rejuvenating powers. With years of sheer dedication and hard work the Company has come a long way in enriching the lives of millions and it hopes to continue this odyssey towards a flourishing world.
